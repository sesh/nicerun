We don't want your data.

Data you upload is processed but is not stored

When you upload a file to Nice Run! your file is processed on our server. The details in the file, including your location data, are read to produce the details needed to generate your image. We do not store the contents of your files, and we do not log the details of your files.

Due to the way that file uploads are processed by our web framework (Django) large files may be temporarily stored. These files are regularly cleared by the system.

Cookies are used for sessions only

Cookies are used to store a session id. We use sessions for rate limiting and for storing temporary data between HTTP requests. No cookies that are not required for the functionality of Run Randomly are used.

Sentry is used for error reporting and performance monitoring

A third-party service called Sentry is used for error reporting and performance monitoring. Your IP address and browser information will be shared with Sentry in order for this service to correctly operate. You can view Sentry's privacy policy for more information.

Mapbox is used to render maps

A third-party service called Mapbox is used to render maps. Your IP address and other browser information is transfered to Mapbox when your browser makes requests for the map data. You can view Mapbox's privacy policy for more information about what is stored.
